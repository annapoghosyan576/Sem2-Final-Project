POMODORO METHOD

Helps maintain focus and prevent burnout.

  1. Set a timer for 25 minutes and focus on studying without distractions.
  2. Take a 5-minute break after the session.
  3. Repeat this cycle 4 times, then take a longer break (15-30 minutes).

ACTIVE RECALL

Strengthens long-term memory and understanding.

  1. Read or review material, then close your notes and try to recall key points from memory.
  2. Write down or say the information out loud.
  3. Check for accuracy and repeat the process, focusing on areas where you struggled.

MIND MAPPING

Great for visual learners and organizing complex information.

  1. Start with a central topic or concept in the middle of a page.
  2. Draw branches extending from it, each representing a key idea or subtopic.
  3. Use keywords, images, and colors to make connections between concepts.

FEYMAN TECHNIQUE

Ensures deep understanding and helps identify weak areas.

  1. Choose a concept and try to explain it in simple terms, as if teaching a child.
  2. Identify any gaps in your understanding and go back to study them.
  3. Simplify and refine your explanation until it is clear and concise.
